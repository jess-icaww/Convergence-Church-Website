Don't Worry!

We understand that to some, going to church may seem intimidating. So, we want to give the opportunity to seek God in a way that is home-like, welcoming manner.

What do we do midweek?

Our Wednesday gatherings provide a smaller, more intimate space designed to focus on individuals and cultivate a family culture within our church. We use this time not only to worship and maintain the same open-door policy as Ekklesia but also to create opportunities for deeper sharing and prayer. We believe that encountering the Lord should not be limited to Sundays alone, and our Wednesdays offer a dedicated space for intentional, personal encounters with God.
