What is Sunday Service?

Our Sunday Ekklesia gathering is a place for the larger body of Christ to come together and meet under one roof. These meetings primarily consist of singing Christian songs, sitting under Biblical preaching, and fellowship. The main hope is that multiple churches can learn the values of unity and partnership through worshiping together, while also keeping an open-door policy whereby anyone can walk in and feel welcomed.
