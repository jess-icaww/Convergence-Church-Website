Who Are We?

Convergence Church is a non-denominational church plant that set out in 2019 to minister to the New York region, carrying a vision to establish the kingdom of God in various sectors of society. We seek to be a church that helps the lost, broken, and disenfranchised by introducing them to Jesus and teaching them the fundamentals of Christianity. But we also want to be a place where believers can continue to grow in their faith, becoming the salt and light of the earth.

Our Sunday Ekklesia services and Wednesday gatherings are the two primary components of our ministry. Our Sundays place emphasis on the larger, corporate body of Christ and serve to expose us to people and traditions outside of Convergence. Our Wednesdays place emphasis on interpersonal relationships and discipleship, as we spend that time praying for each other and sitting under various teachings. Both meetings are essential to get a holistic, Biblical church experience.
